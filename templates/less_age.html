Age Restriction

For individuals aged 16 years or younger, we cannot calculate a BMI value accurately. Maintaining a healthy lifestyle is important for overall well-being. Here are some tips to help maintain a good BMI value:

  • Engage in regular physical activity, such as walking, jogging, or swimming.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
  • Avoid excessive consumption of sugary and high-fat foods.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Get adequate sleep and manage stress levels effectively.
